Bass Boosted

Bass Boosted is a magazine based on various sub-cultures in music. The contents include feature articles, interviews, puzzles, and insights on new releases. By now, there are three issues of Bass Boosted, distributed free in venues such as Reverie Bookstore and Desperate Infant Records.

Articles include: Bass Interview 4: K-the-I???, When The Pawn Hits The Conflict

Women's Story

Women's Story is a special magazine for the International Women's Day 2025, to highlight the achievements that women has made in our society. Right now, Women's Story can be found at the reception desk of Nord Anglia International School Hong Kong. Look forward to the next issue!

Sunday Lettuce

Sunday Lettuce was a surprisingly good emulation of satarical newspapers, mainly The Onions. Readers get to know about the daily lives of vegetables, as well as the economic and sociopolitical insight of the green planet. The issues of Sunday Lettuce were given at Nord Anglia International School Hong Kong's spring fair as a freshly printed A3 paper, served beside the delicious cups of carrot juice.
